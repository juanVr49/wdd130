Dry Oar Rafting Co.

We love being on the river and helping other people enjoy it as much as we do. At Dry Oar, our mission is to create unforgettable memories and build lifelong friendships through safe, thrilling, and meaningful outdoor experiences.

History

Dry Oar Rafting Co. began with a simple idea: make river running accessible to everyone. Over the years, we’ve grown from a small group of passionate rafters to a trusted outfitter, providing world-class trips across some of the most beautiful rivers in the region. Our team is committed to safety, fun, and genuine connection with every guest who joins us.
